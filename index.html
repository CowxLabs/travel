Travel Tips

💰

Budget Travel

Travel during shoulder season for lower prices and fewer crowds. Use local transport.

🌍

Packing Light

One carry-on is enough. Pack versatile layers and do laundry on the road.

📷

Photo Spots

Golden hour (sunrise/sunset) gives the best light. Avoid midday harsh shadows.
